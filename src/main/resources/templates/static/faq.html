How do I save my days?

You write six words that describes your day in the best way. There are two available input types: you can either write six separate words or one sentence composed of six words. For each word/sentence you also choose whether it is positive, neutral or negative feeling.

Can I change my input type?

Sure, you can do this but only once per month. So you need to be convinced of it, otherwise you will have to wait another month before next input type changing.

Who can see my diary?

It depends on you who you want to share your days with. You choose one of four options in profile settings:
- Public - allows everyone to see your diary
- Protected - only logged in users can see your diary
- For selected - you share your diary with users who you select in "share" section
- Private - you don't share your diary at all

I'd like to make my profile public but there are few entries that I would like to hide. Is it possible?

Yes, there is an option to choose for each day if you'd like to make it private, protected, for selected or public.

However, this function doesn't work reverse. You are able to hide some of your days but if your profile is hidden you cannot share particular days.

Can I edit my entry after saving the day?

You can edit entries from specific 3 days, starting from the current day and ending with 2 days before it.

Although, you can always change the sharing style of the day.

What if I happen to forget to save my day?

No problem! You can always complete days from the past.

Additionally, we will send you an email about your absence. You can choose yourself how often you would like us to send reminders.

Can I change my username/e-mail?

Yes, you can do it in account settings. Yes, anytime you want :)

I did reset my password/create an account but I wasn't sent any email...

Most likely it is in your SPAM folder. However, if you still can't find it just send us a message by feedback form so we can work it out together.

Can I delete my account?

No, there is no such option. But still and all, you are able to deactive it in settings.

I have deactivated my account but I want to resume writing in Six Words Diary. Do I have to register again?

There is no need to do this. You can easily reactivate your account by logging in as usual. This way you can easily get back to your old diary without losing any entries.
